ABOUT OUR COMPANY

With over a decade of experience in the automotive industry, CRAS has built a reputation for excellence, reliability, and customer satisfaction. Our passion for cars drives everything we do.

OUR STORY

Founded in 2014, CRAS Automotive began as a small garage with a simple mission: to provide honest, high-quality automotive services to our community. What started with just two technicians has grown into a full-service automotive center with over 20 certified professionals.

Our commitment to excellence, cutting-edge technology, and customer-first approach has made us the trusted choice for thousands of vehicle owners across the region. We believe that every car deserves the best care, and every customer deserves transparent, reliable service.

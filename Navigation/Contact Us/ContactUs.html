FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by calling us at (406) 555-0120, using our online appointment form on the website, or visiting our facility in person during business hours.

What services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of automotive services including routine maintenance, engine repair, performance modifications, interior and exterior customization, detailing, and diagnostic services. Visit our Services page for detailed information.

Do you provide warranty on repairs?

Yes, we provide a 12-month/12,000-mile warranty on all repairs and services. For certain modifications and parts, we offer extended warranty options. Our team will discuss warranty details before starting any work.

What are your payment options?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), cash, and checks. We also offer financing options through our partner lenders for larger repairs and modifications.

How long will my repair take?

Repair time depends on the complexity of the work needed. Simple services like oil changes can be completed in under an hour, while major repairs may take several days. We'll provide you with an estimated completion time before starting the work.

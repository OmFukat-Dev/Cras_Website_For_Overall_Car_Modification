📅 Appointments & Scheduling

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can book an appointment online via our Appointment page, call us at (406) 555-0120, or visit our facility in person during business hours. Online bookings are available 24/7.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes. Please call us at least 24 hours before your appointment to reschedule or cancel without any penalty. You can reach us at 1-800-915-6271 or email us at example@email.com.

How long will my repair take?

Service time varies by complexity. Simple services like oil changes take under an hour. Engine diagnostics and brake work typically take 1–3 hours. Major repairs or performance modifications may take 1–5 days. We'll give you a time estimate before starting.

Do you offer same-day service?

Yes, for many routine services like oil changes, tire rotations, and minor diagnostics. Call us in the morning and we'll do our best to accommodate you the same day. For complex repairs, we recommend scheduling in advance.

🔧 Services & Repairs

What services do you offer?

We offer a full range of automotive services including engine diagnostics & repair, performance tuning, interior & exterior modifications, ceramic coating, vinyl wraps, detailing, brake & suspension work, ECU remapping, turbo/supercharger installation, and fleet maintenance. Visit our Services page for full details.

Do you work on all vehicle makes and models?

Yes. Our certified technicians are trained to work on all makes and models including domestic, European, Japanese, and Korean vehicles. We have experience with everything from daily drivers to high-performance sports cars and commercial fleets.

Do you provide a warranty on repairs?

Yes. We provide a 12-month / 12,000-mile warranty on all repairs and services. Certain modifications and parts may come with extended manufacturer warranties. Our team will clearly explain warranty coverage before starting any work.

Can you do performance modifications on any car?

Our performance specialists can work on most vehicles. We'll consult with you first to ensure the modifications are appropriate for your specific vehicle, driving goals, and budget. We always prioritize safety and reliability in all performance work.

💳 Pricing & Payment

How are prices determined?

We provide upfront, transparent pricing based on the service type, parts required, and labor time. You'll always receive a detailed written estimate before we begin any work. There are no hidden fees — what we quote is what you pay. View our Pricing page for service packages.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), cash, and personal checks. We also offer in-house financing for services over $500 with terms from 6 to 36 months at competitive APR rates.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes! We offer flexible in-house financing for larger service packages starting at $500. Terms range from 6 to 36 months with APR starting from 4.99%. Same-day approval is available. Visit our Pricing & Finance page for details.

Are there any current promotions or discounts?

Yes! We regularly run seasonal promotions. New customers get $100 off any service package over $500 (code: WELCOME100). We also have a referral bonus program — earn $150 credit for every friend you refer. Check our Pricing page for current offers.

📍 Location & Hours

Where are you located?

We are located at 901 N Pitt Street, Suite 170, Alexandria, NY 13607, USA. We have ample parking and a comfortable customer lounge with complimentary refreshments while you wait.

What are your business hours?

Monday–Friday: 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM | Saturday: 9:00 AM – 4:00 PM | Sunday: Closed. Emergency roadside assistance is available 24/7. Call 1-800-915-6271 for emergencies.

Do you offer pickup and delivery?

Yes. For qualifying services, we offer vehicle pickup and delivery within a 15-mile radius of our facility. This service is available Monday through Friday. Please mention it when scheduling your appointment.

🚗 Buying a Vehicle

Do you sell used cars?

Yes! We have a certified pre-owned vehicle program. Every vehicle goes through a 150-point inspection and comes with a 12-month/12,000-mile warranty. Browse our Buy Car page to see available inventory.

Can I trade in my vehicle?

Absolutely. We offer free vehicle appraisals and provide fair market value for trade-ins. The credit can be applied toward any service package or vehicle purchase. Contact us to schedule a trade-in appraisal.

— PRICING FAQ —

GOT QUESTIONS?

Everything you need to know about our pricing, packages, and payment options.

Are there any hidden fees in the listed prices? +

Absolutely not. At CRAS Automotive, transparency is a core value. You'll always receive a detailed written estimate before we start any work, and the final invoice will never exceed what was quoted without explicit approval from you. No surprises, ever.

Can I customize a package to fit my exact needs? +

Yes! Our listed packages are starting points. You can add or remove specific services, mix and match from different categories, or build a fully custom quote. Contact us or book an appointment for a free consultation with one of our service advisors.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex, Discover), cash, and personal checks. We also offer in-house financing for services over $500 with terms from 6–36 months at APR rates from 4.99%.

How do I qualify for financing? +

Financing is available for services totaling $500 or more. Same-day approvals are the norm. Our finance team will review your application with no hard credit pull for pre-qualification. Visit our Appointment page or call us at 1-800-915-6271 to get started.

Do the packages include a warranty? +

Yes. All service packages come with our standard 12-month / 12,000-mile warranty on labor and installed parts. Some modifications (e.g., leather seats, ceramic coating) carry extended manufacturer warranties of 3–10 years. Your advisor will explain coverage in detail.

Can I get a quote before committing? +

Of course! Use the Payment Calculator above for a quick estimate, or book a free in-person or virtual consultation. We'll inspect your vehicle, discuss your goals, and provide a full written quote with zero obligation to proceed.
